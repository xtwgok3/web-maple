NOOB GUIDE

Newbie Guide:

  1. Turn off your Anti-Virus and then Download All files (Client, Launcher & others).

  2. Install MapleStory Client.

  3. Go to your MapleStory Folder, e.g., C:\Nexon\MapleStory\

  4. Delete the folder HShield, Aspxxx.exe, MapleStory.exe, Patcher.

  5. Copy your downloaded Launcher "Maple.exe" and paste it in your MapleStory Folder.

    6. (apply this just in case your game wont run)

  6. Right-click on Maple.exe, go to:

    Propieties> Compatibility> Run Compatibility> (SELECT) Windows 8> Check the box "Run As Administrator" and Resolution 640x480.

Enjoy Mappling~
